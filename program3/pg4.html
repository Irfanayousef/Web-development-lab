ICET

ICET college" refers to an institution that offers MBA or MCA programs through admission via the **Telangana Integrated Common Entrance Test (TG ICET) ** or the Andhra Pradesh Integrated Common Entrance Test (AP ICET). These are common entrance exams for undergraduate degrees in the states of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh, respectively, and the colleges are those that have been authorized to accept students based on their ICET ranks.
