Department of Computer Application

A Department of Computer Applications (DCA) offers specialized education in applying computer science to solve practical problems, with a focus on software development, data management, and emerging technologies like AI and Data Science. These departments provide courses, often at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels (e.g., Bachelor of Computer Application - BCA, and Master of Computer Application - MCA), to produce skilled professionals for the IT industry. They emphasize hands-on experience through labs and projects, preparing students for careers in developing and managing software and managing complex information systems.
